Paul Kugelman

Sean did a fantastic job with the sale of my house in April of 2010. He provided top-notch strategic advice as to exactly when to list the house for sale so that I could leverage the recent buyers' tax credit deadline to my advantage. The result: a higher price, faster sale and better negotiating position than if it had been listed earlier or later. My house was under contract in six days, and I received more for it than other comparable houses recently sold in my townhouse development. He also provided great advice as to staging the house to make it look its best; I almost didn't want to sell it once I saw how great it looked!
